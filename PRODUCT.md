# BookEat Mobile Product Context

BookEat is a transactional restaurant-booking product for customers, restaurant owners, and administrators. The React Native app is the customer-facing companion to the existing web and backend systems. Customers discover restaurants, reserve tables, pay deposits, manage bookings, cancel under a clearly stated policy, receive eligible refunds into BookEat Wallet, and reuse wallet balance on later bookings.

The product should feel trustworthy, premium, calm, and financially transparent. Money-affecting actions must show exact amounts, status, consequences, and a clear next step. Cancellation calculations and wallet balances always come from the backend; the mobile UI must not independently infer financial results.

Primary mobile users are diners who need a fast booking flow and an obvious record of deposits, cancellation fees, refunds, wallet usage, and transaction history. Touch targets, contrast, loading/error states, Vietnamese copy, and safe handling of repeated taps are required.

Avoid demo-only controls, fake coin simulators, generic dashboard styling, hidden fees, unexplained balance changes, and destructive actions without explicit confirmation.
